Technology X Healthcare

We simplify access to informed healthcare

View our approach
close

Access Issue

  • The lack of accurate and up-to-date health information hinders healthcare providers' ability to deliver quality care in Kenya.
  • Without accurate information, individuals may not be aware of the available healthcare services, leading to limited access and missed opportunities for timely care.
  • Patients and caregivers face challenges in making informed decisions about their health due to a lack of accurate information on treatment options, preventive measures, and available resources.
  • The lack of accurate information exacerbates health disparities, with marginalized communities often having limited access to reliable healthcare information, leading to unequal access to quality care.
  • Inaccurate information can contribute to poor health outcomes, as individuals may not receive appropriate care, timely interventions, or preventive measures based on their specific needs.

Our Solution

  • Our healthcare app provides accurate and up-to-date health information, empowering users with reliable resources to make informed decisions about their care.
  • The app ensures that users have comprehensive knowledge of available healthcare services, including details about nearby facilities, services offered, and insurance covers accepted.
  • The app enables users to locate nearby healthcare facilities, reducing delays in accessing care and ensuring prompt attention to their health needs.
  • Our app helps people save more by providing information on facilities that offer affordable services, highlighting cost-effective options without compromising quality care.
  • Users can easily access information about healthcare facilities that accept specific insurance covers, facilitating seamless utilization of their insurance benefits and reducing out-of-pocket expenses.
